Our Story

The Team Behind OpExec Group

Who We Are

Founded in 2023, we are proud to be a female-owned and operated small business. According to Gartner, "In 2023, 26% of executive level positions in the supply chain were held by women, with women representing 41% of the industry workforce overall". We are extremely proud and humbled to be part of that 26% and will continue mentoring and sharing our experience in an effort to grow that number in coming years.

At OpExec Group, we are passionate about helping companies grow and succeed. With over 20 years of experience in supply chain and operations executive leadership, we have the expertise to help businesses navigate the challenges of growth.

Our team of experts have a proven track record of helping businesses achieve their goals and reach new heights.

Why Choose OpExec Group

Our Experience

OpExec Group has over 20 years of experience in the supply chain and operations field and is passionate about helping businesses like yours. We'll assess your needs, implement solutions, and help you navigate the unknowns that come with the pivotal time between the start of your business and scaled growth. Partnering with us will bring a sense of security during this exciting time.

Our Approach

At OpExec Group, we bring a passion for helping businesses succeed. As the founder, I saw a need for personalized service for start-up to mid-sized companies that had the vision to bring a product to life but did not have experience in product development or supply chain and operational management.

We approach your needs as if they were our own. We start by learning about your company and understanding what passion drives your business. For us to fully engage in creating a personalized plan, we want to "feel" what brought the company to life.
